School Classes

Our school variety of fun and interactive classes designed to match every child's age and learning stage. Through engaging activities, creative play, and guided lessons, children develop essential academic and social skills in a joyful and supportive environtment.

KG A

KG A class is designed to develop children's basic skills in reading, writing, counting, and social interaction through fun and educational activities.

KG B

KG B class prepares children for elementary school by focusing on academic readiness, discipline, and responsibility.

PG A

PG A class focuses on stimulating children's sensory and motor development.

PG B

PG B class helps children become more independent and confident through enjoyable and structured activities.

After-School Programme

Our after-school classes offer children the opportunity to explore their interests and talents beyond regular lessons. These programs help students develop creativity, confidence in a fun and supportive environment.

Robotics / Coding

The Robotics program is designed to develop children's foundational skills in reading, writing, counting, and social interaction through fun and educational activities.

Calistung

The Calistung program is designed to build children's foundational skills in reading, writing, and counting through fun and engaging activities.

English

The English program is designed to introduce children to the English language through interactive storytelling, songs, and daily conversations in a fun and supportive environment.

Mathematic

The Mathematic program is designed to develop children's logical thinking and number sense through hands-on activities, games, and visual learning experiences.

Sensoric Class

The Sensoric program is designed to stimulate children's sensory development through creative exploration of textures, sounds, colors, and movements in a playful and nurturing setting.
