About Kasih Paramitha School

Established in 2016 under the Vidya Parahita Foundation, Kasih Paramitha School is located in West Tarakan, North Kalimantan and focuses on early childhood education.

Our approach encourages curiosity and creativity through hands-on, theme-based learning inspired fun-based methodologies.

English is the primary language of instruction, enriched with Mandarin and Bahasa Indonesia.

Vision & Mission

Vision

We value development of every child's personal attribute to have confident, critical, skillful and thoughtful generation in becoming a part of a future global citizenship.

Mission

We will empower every student to develop their fullest potential, critical thinking, character building, equipped with communicative and technology skills by engaging them in a lifelong learning process to face a changing global community.
